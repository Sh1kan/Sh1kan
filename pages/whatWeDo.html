Social Media – an integral part of the day to day live of almost every client of yours. Are you on it? Do you have well targeted campaigns with clear objectives? If your answer is no, you might need to start thinking about it. But starting a Social Media Marketing Campaign without a strategy in mind is like wandering around a forest without a map – you might have fun, but you’ll most probably get lost. 

This is why, when we are executing campaigns, we are going to answer together with you the following questions:

What is the goal of the campaign? 

Who is our target audience? 

Where would our target audience hang out and how would they use social media? 

What message do we want to send to our audience trough the social media? 

After that, we will be ready to create the perfect Social Media Marketing Plan, so that we can reach the goals in the most efficient and effective way!
